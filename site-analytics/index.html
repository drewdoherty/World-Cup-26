Analytics Dashboard // The Full Paper Book, Not Just The Bets
Every priced model selection is treated as a paper bet — persisted, settled, and CLV-stamped whether or not money moved. This kills prediction-selection bias: the realized book is a strict subset of the paper book. CLV is the lower-variance leading edge signal; ROI is the lagging confirmation; calibration is the mechanism check. Honesty about sample size is a first-class feature — the default verdict is INSUFFICIENT SAMPLE until power gates clear.
