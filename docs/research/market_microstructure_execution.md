# World Cup Alpha — Market Microstructure & Execution Engine

## 1. Executive Summary

**Read the data reality first — it caps every number in this document.**

- **One source.** All 1,262,293 price rows in `data/wca.db.odds_snapshots` come from **TheOddsAPI only**. There is no Pinnacle, no direct exchange stream, and no independent sharp reference. Every "fair" / "consensus" line here is built from ~20 books inside that one feed, so it is internally consistent but **not externally validated**.
- **Four markets.** `h2h` (1X2, 1,097,599 rows), `totals` (135,746), `btts` (25,924), `h2h_lay` (exchange lay, 3,024). **No Asian-handicap, no correct-score, no corners/cards, no prop prices** exist — those cross-checks are framework-only by absence of data.
- **12-day pre-tournament window, 72 matches** (2026-06-11..06-23), ~315 capture times; 65 matches have a deep ~311-snapshot intraday series at ~3.2-min median cadence.
- **The decisive structural gap: capture STOPPED on 2026-06-23, before most matches kick off.** For the median deep match the last in-window snapshot is **~38–42 hours before kickoff**. Only **8 of 72** matches have any snapshot within 1h of KO; **5 of 65** deep matches within 6h. **There is effectively no true closing line in this dataset.** This single fact caps price-discovery convergence, CLV measurement, and movement-prediction — all are gated to small "true-close" subsets (n=8–11) and are labelled *indicative* or *framework-only* accordingly.
- **Cadence ≠ latency.** All books share ONE ~3-min API capture grid; real cross-venue transmission is sub-second. So lead/lag and "staleness" are measured at multi-minute granularity only — they say nothing about true tick latency and **cannot be used to claim a latency-arb edge**.
- **The ledger CLV→P&L link is n=25** (of 69 settled bets carrying `closing_odds`+`clv`). That is far below decision-grade. Its CLV→ROI correlation is **negative (-0.28) and is pure small-sample noise** — not evidence that beating the close hurts.
- **No Polymarket price history.** Only 10 `pm_parked` + 6 `pm_order_log` rows (0 live fills, all dry-run). All PM combo work rests on ONE live cross-section (`site/advancement_data.json`), so it is **framework-only**, never realised edge.

**The honest verdict.** The dominant, reproducible, robust finding across all ten verified areas is **structural, not predictive**: exchanges price the 1X2 at ~0–2% overround vs a median ~6.8% sportsbook margin, with classic favourite-longshot bias (18–52% longshot margin vs ~3–7% on favourites). That gap is a **cost-reduction edge** (venue routing + line-shopping + negative-avoidance), not alpha. Every *active* timing/prediction edge tested — lead/lag, disagreement→movement, movement-sign prediction, consensus-beats-best-book, synthetic BTTS arbitrage — came back **null or unmeasurable on this data**. The cross-book arbitrage that survives commission is real but thin (~61 episodes in 12 days, median net margin 0.36%) and dominated by execution friction we cannot yet measure (depth, latency, gubbing). So the engineering payoff here is an **execution engine that captures the structural cost edge cheaply and safely**, plus a **validation harness** that re-tests every active edge once odds capture is fixed to run to kickoff.

### Edge details, basis, and the corrections folded in

- **#1 Venue routing.** Median sportsbook 1X2 overround **6.78%** vs exchange median **0.70%**; widest book **betfair_sb_uk 14.07%** (n=43, indicative). FLB: longshot proportional margin **18–52%** vs favourite ~3–7% (4–13×). *Verifier correction applied:* the **stalest full-season sportsbook is betfair_sb_uk at 0.269× the consensus reprice rate** (williamhill 0.405× is second; williamhill only "stalest" if restricted to n=72 books). Leadership is a cadence proxy, not true latency, and only **matchbook (0.27) + betfair_ex (0.17) + betvictor (0.31)** lead most — **smarkets (0.11) does NOT stand out**. EV is *mostly* negative-avoidance + line-shopping (a separate, partly already-captured edge), not active value-betting.
- **#2 Liquidity.** Spreads are **displayed top-of-book** (cross-to-mid = half-spread idealisation; no depth-aware fill guarantee). Lay capture is sparse (7 timestamps, 2026-06-13..17, 3020 matched pairs) so venue/time breakdowns are **indicative**; the headline median and venue ordering reproduced exactly under independent SQL. **Depth, matched volume, queue position, market impact are NOT measurable** from TheOddsAPI — framework-only with a capture schema specified.
- **#3 Arbitrage.** The eye-catching double-digit "arbs" (70% of in-play snapshots, up to 798%) are **post-goal asynchronous-repricing artifacts** and are correctly excluded. 65% of net arbs need a commission-netted exchange leg; the all-sportsbook 35% are exposed to stake limits / gubbing not in the margin math. *Verifier note:* a single ts_utc cycle bundles fetches spanning a median ~76s (max ~15min), so a "matched-timestamp" arb can be a couple of minutes stale — at 0.36% median margin this matters and is disclosed.
- **#4 CLV.** Exchange close-vs-consensus tightness (betfair_ex −1.24%, matchbook −1.23%, smarkets −1.74%) is **pre-commission**; net of Smarkets 0% / Betfair ~2–5%, the "no beatable close exists" thesis only strengthens. Realised ledger n=25; the −0.28 CLV→ROI correlation is driven by a single Smarkets Draw winner (CLV −29.7%, +£43.50, ROI +174%) — variance, not signal.

### Wiring notes (load-bearing)

- **De-vig + fair price** everywhere uses `markets/devig.py` — `shin()` for 3-way 1X2, `multiplicative()` for totals (matches the synthetic/consensus methodology). Do not re-implement.
- **Net price after commission** is `arb.effective_back(odds, commission)`; PM legs use `arb.pm_yes_to_decimal` with the `PM_TAKER_FEE_RATE` (0.03·p·(1−p)). The arb scan/rank is `arb.find_cross_book_arbs` + `arb.rank_arbs`.
- **Correlated sizing** is `exposure_corr.build_correlated_exposure` over the `scoreline_matrix` — this is the one place that prevents treating correlated re-expressions of the goal supply as diversified, the central Phase-2 risk.
- **Kelly / simultaneous exposure** scaling is `markets/kelly.simultaneous_exposure_scale` + `KellyPolicy` (quarter-Kelly default per Phase-2).
- **Position truth** across venues is `positions_sync.py` (currently dry-run); failure recovery reads/writes through it. Never let placement default to live without the safety ladder.

### Prerequisite data capture (HARD gate)

The engine can do **venue ranking, negative-avoidance, line-shopping, the sanity filter, and correlated exposure today** with TheOddsAPI top-of-book. Everything stake-sensitive — SOR sizing, slippage curve, queue positioning, market impact, and any arb above tiny stakes — is **blocked until** a depth/volume feed exists:

1. **Betfair Exchange Stream API** — `EX_ALL_OFFERS` (full ladder depth) + `EX_TRADED` (matched volume), timestamped at ingest.
2. **Smarkets depth** (or Matchbook) for the tightest displayed venue.
3. **To-kickoff capture** (fix the daemon so it does not stop pre-KO) — prerequisite for a true closing line, which unblocks the CLV, convergence, movement, consensus, and disagreement re-tests in Section 3.

Proposed schema (`exchange_depth`): `(ts_utc, source, match_id, market, selection, side, level, price, size)` + `(... , matched_volume)` for traded.

## 5. Roadmap

Phased to front-load high-certainty / low-effort wins, gated on the validation harness, and consistent with `docs/research/phase2_research_program.md` (whose Wave-1 already includes the correlated-exposure model, calibration dashboard, and walk-forward CLV harness — this roadmap **extends** that program with the microstructure layer rather than duplicating it).

**Phase 0 — Fix the measurement floor (prerequisite, no betting).**
- **Run odds capture uninterrupted to kickoff** for every match. This is the single biggest data fix: it creates a true closing line and unblocks the CLV, convergence, movement-prediction, consensus, and disagreement re-tests that are currently null/unmeasurable purely because capture stopped 2026-06-23.
- Stand up the **walk-forward CLV harness** (Phase-2 Wave-1 item) on cached + new snapshots — the gate every active edge must pass.
- *Gate:* harness runs forward-only with no look-ahead and reproduces the published microstructure numbers.

**Phase 1 — Capture the structural cost edge (high-certainty, low-effort, no new market data).**
- **Venue selector + line-shopping + negative-avoidance** (Section 4): always route to the lowest-margin venue, never bet into betfair_sb_uk 14% or 36–52% longshot lines. Reuses `venues.py`, `devig.py`, `arb.effective_back`.
- **Whole-book correlated exposure guard** via `exposure_corr.build_correlated_exposure` — size 1X2+totals(+BTTS) on a fixture as ONE bucket vs the hard cash floor.
- **Synthetic-pricing sanity filter** (block prices off the goal-supply fair value by more than book vig).
- *Gate:* on a held-out tournament half, the book-weakness ranking (margin/FLB/staleness) is Spearman-stable across disjoint match folds.

**Phase 2 — Depth capture, then liquidity-aware execution.**
- Build the **Betfair/Smarkets depth + matched-volume capture** and the `exchange_depth` schema.
- Turn on the **smart order router, liquidity-aware sizer, depth-walk slippage model, and partial-fill failure recovery** (all blocked until depth exists).
- *Gate:* depth-aware paper-fills to a shadow ledger show realised slippage within the modelled band, and latency-survival of re-fetched legs is measured (not assumed).

**Phase 3 — Arbitrage, gated on Phase 2.**
- Activate the **commission-survived cross-book arb scan** (`arb.find_cross_book_arbs`/`rank_arbs`) with the depth + latency-survival haircut baked in; shadow-ledger every flagged arb before any live stake; track gubbing on the sportsbook leg over weeks.
- *Gate:* shadow-ledger realised return stays positive net of depth, latency, and gubbing across >30 out-of-sample episodes.

**Phase 4 — Re-test the active edges (only if the harness shows signal).**
- With a true closing line, re-run the Section-3 nulls (lead-lag at finer cadence with a tick feed, disagreement→movement, movement-sign, convergence-timing, consensus, synthetic) on train/test splits. Promote **only** edges that beat an honest baseline OOS with a bootstrap CI excluding zero.
- Add **PM advancement daily snapshots** to make the combo decomposition gradeable (≥40 resolved ties) before any PM combo capital.
- *Gate:* each promoted edge clears its pre-registered OOS test; otherwise it stays a diagnostic, not an objective.

## 6. Honesty Notes

- **No true closing line exists in this data.** Capture stopped 2026-06-23; median last-snapshot-to-KO gap ~38–42h; only 8/72 matches within 1h of KO. Every "close"-based number is a stale pre-KO proxy and is labelled indicative/framework where it depends on the true close.
- **Single source (TheOddsAPI).** No Pinnacle/independent sharp reference; all "fair"/"consensus" lines are internally consistent only. "Beatable early prices" is a margin-tightness ranking, NOT line-prediction alpha.
- **Cadence ≠ latency.** ~3-min capture grid; staleness/lead-lag are multi-minute-relative, not tick-latency. No latency-arb claim is supported.
- **Ledger CLV→P&L is n=25** — far below significance; the −0.28 correlation is noise driven by one high-odds winner on a negative-CLV bet. CLV is a diagnostic, not yet an optimisation objective.
- **Depth/volume/queue/market-impact are NOT in the data.** All execution-sizing components (SOR, slippage curve, queue positioning, impact) are framework-only until Betfair/Smarkets depth streaming is captured; until then, hard-cap to small stakes.
- **No Polymarket price history.** Combo/advancement work is one live cross-section (`advancement_data.json`) — framework-only, never realised edge; the 3 "coherence violations" are sub-1% minnow quote noise, likely not lockable.
- **Most EV figures are framework estimates on a ~£3k bankroll, not backtested P&L**, and are dominated by execution friction (latency, gubbing, depth). The dependable edge is cost-reduction (routing/line-shopping), not alpha.
- **Verifier corrections folded in:** price-discovery T-6h/T-1h convergence is a coverage artifact (genuinely-observed ~40%/~19%, not 100%/85%); near-T-1h coverage is 2 matches not 3; stalest full-season sportsbook is betfair_sb_uk (0.269×), not williamhill; exchange leadership is matchbook+betfair_ex+betvictor (smarkets does NOT lead); movement-prediction's 65.1% baseline is an oracle (honest baseline ties the model at 58.1%); synthetic rho-band is 9/21 exceed / 12/21 within; arb "matched-timestamp" cycles span ~76s of fetches.
- **n labels are literal:** measured = full-population census or robustly reproduced; indicative = n<30 or coverage-limited; framework-only = no data exists, method spec only.
